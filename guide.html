User Guide

Thank you for playing 'All That Quiz'. This guide explains more about the quizzes and the website overall. Have fun!

1. Navigating the Website

Use the navigation bar at the top of the page to go to the starting page or for the page describing this website.

2. Taking a Quiz

  1. Click on the 'Next' button at the bottom of this page to be transported to our genres of quizzes.
  2. Choose among the three genres of 'Entertainment', 'History', and 'Science'.

3. Answering Questions

  • Each question will have multiple choice options. Select the answer you think is correct.
  • Once you have answered all the questions, your score will be revealed to you.
